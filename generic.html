About me

Hi, my name is Katelyn Horstman (she/her) and I attended UCLA, where I graduated with departmental honors and received a Bachelor of Science in Astrophysics in 2021. Currently, I am pursuing my PhD in Astrophysics at Caltech, working with Professor Dimitri Mawet in the Exoplanet Technology (ET) lab. My current research interests include exomoon detection, exoplanet detection and characterization, and near-infrared instrumentation.
